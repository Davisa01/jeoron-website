The Pest Management Professionals

A Trusted Name in Nigeria’s Pest Control Industry

Jeoron Fumigation is on a mission to raise the standard of pest control and environmental management across Nigeria. We provide safe, reliable, and effective fumigation services for homes, offices, schools, hospitals, warehouses, and industries — protecting lives, properties, and businesses.

Since our establishment, Jeoron has earned a reputation for professionalism and results. From eradicating household pests to large-scale industrial fumigation, our team ensures every project is handled with precision, safety, and care.

While our operations are proudly based in Nigeria, our vision is to set the benchmark for pest management solutions, expanding our services to meet growing needs and contributing to a healthier, pest-free environment for all.

We are trusted by property managers, schools, hotels, and private clients who rely on Jeoron for consistent, effective pest control solutions.

Our Professional Services

At Jeoron Fumigation, we are committed to protecting homes, businesses, and communities across Nigeria with reliable pest control, fumigation, and hygiene solutions. Our services are designed to ensure safety, health, and peace of mind — no matter the scale of your needs.
