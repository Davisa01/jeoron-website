About Jeoron

At Jeoron Concepts Nig Ltd, we are committed to providing quality veterinary medical products that support the health and productivity of animals across Nigeria and beyond. With a passion for animal wellness and a dedication to service, we supply veterinary pharmaceuticals, supplements, and health solutions that meet both local and international standards.

Our products are sourced from trusted manufacturers and tailored to the unique needs of veterinarians, livestock farmers, poultry owners, and pet clinics. We believe in responsible distribution, continuous learning, and building lasting partnerships with every client we serve.

Premium Fumigation & Pest Control Services

Protecting Your Home, Farm, and Business from Harmful Pests

Jeoron Concepts Nig Ltd offers safe, eco-friendly, and highly effective fumigation services to keep your environment clean and pest-free. We handle everything from insects and rodents to harmful pathogens, using modern techniques that guarantee results.
